# Hult Prize College Level Competition

## Overview
This repository contains the presentation and supporting documents for the Hult Prize College Level Competition. The project focuses on extracting ethanol from algae as a sustainable and environmentally friendly business solution.

## Project Title
Ethanol Production from Algae for Sustainable Business Development

## Problem Statement
The increasing demand for energy and the environmental impact of fossil fuels require alternative renewable energy sources. There is a need for sustainable fuel production methods that reduce carbon emissions and support green economic growth.

## Proposed Solution
This project proposes the extraction of ethanol from algae as a renewable biofuel. Algae is a fast-growing and sustainable resource that can be cultivated without competing with food crops. The ethanol produced can be used as an alternative fuel source, contributing to environmental protection and sustainable business opportunities.

## Key Objectives
- Promote renewable energy solutions  
- Develop a sustainable biofuel production model  
- Reduce environmental pollution  
- Create economic opportunities through green entrepreneurship  

## Repository Contents
- `Hult.pptx` – Project presentation slides  
- Competition certificates  
- Supporting documentation related to participation  

## Impact
The project highlights the potential of algae-based ethanol as a clean energy solution. It supports sustainable development goals by encouraging innovation, environmental responsibility, and green business practices.
